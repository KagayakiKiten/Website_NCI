The Four Pillars of NanoTech Indonesia

Natural

Providing services to partners naturally, with high integrity and honesty

Agile

Identify problems and opportunities, move to follow up, and do both iteratively without stopping and with short periods.

Novelty

The company always updates, plans, and develops the latest material engineering-based technology that customers need.

Optimistic

Enthusiasm with positive expectations for the future, creating a work culture that builds each other so as to be able to provide the best services for customers.

Vision

Directing strong, responsible, and quality research and development units from the perspective of physics and chemistry

Mission

1. Providing the APC research and development division with nanotechnology-based laboratory facilities equipped with the latest and most advanced component infrastructure.

2. Providing consulting services for innovative products, particularly the study of nanomaterial characterization and utilization, in order to respond to market challenges from both a scientific and an applied standpoint.

3. Actively cooperate and support each other with other CoEs in the structure chart of the Nano Center Indonesia foundation.

4. Development of capacity and quality of CoE APC human resources that are rapidly growing, sophisticated, and with integrity.

Our Milestone

  1. 2005

    Establishment of nanotechnology and advanced material research groups at LIPI Physics

  2. 2009

    CV Nanotech Indonesia was established to commercialize their first nanoparticle equipment in Indonesia.

  3. 2012

    Nano Center Indonesia Foundation was established as a center for research, development of nanotechnology-based products.

  4. 2019

    The establishment PT Nanotech Indonesia Global to manage the business of the Indonesian nanotech group as a provider of innovative technology.

  5. 2022

    Applied Physics and Chemistry is now a part of Nano Center Indonesia to conducting research and develop a nanomaterial products.
