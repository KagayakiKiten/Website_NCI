Research Area

We develop solutions for the study of complex scientific and engineering problems. Research areas in our lab are in: surface chemistry and nano-based delivery system applied to areas of Pharmaceutical, Personal Care, Nanotechnology and their interfaces. We emphasize the parallel development of tools, method, hardware and applications while we consider indispensable the interactions with our collaborators.

Nanobubble

Fine bubble technology is a technology for modifying the size of a gas in a liquid to a fine size (less than 100 micrometers) It can provide unique properties of bubbles in liquids as their stability in water compared to macro bubbles

Nanofiber

Nanofiber is a fiber structure with range 1 nanometer to 1 micrometer size diameter The synthesis of nanofibers aims to obtain the benefits of physical and chemical properties that has more advantages than bulk material

Nanopetro

The bubbles used are in the nanoscale, so the stability of the resulting foam is very high on high stability foam can be applied to oil and gas mining to push petroleum out

Hybrid

The textile industry is notorious for its water consumption and, consequently, the wastewater that it generatesTherefore research on the effects of ultrasonic radiation on wastewater has been progressing
